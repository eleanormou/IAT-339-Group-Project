Care instructions

Bronze and silver naturally oxidize over time. This grey hue, a chemical reaction, can be removed with a metal polish and a polishing cloth. To delay oxidation, keep your Jeu Jewelry away from moisture, shower steam, and other water activities. And when you’re not wearing your jewelry, store it in a plastic bag.
